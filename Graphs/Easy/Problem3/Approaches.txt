1. Approach 1 :  using degree of an vertex (Intuitive approach )
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Maintain the indegree and outdegree of vertices
2. As judge does not trust anyone and everybody trust judge we, will increment trust point (A->B)  to B and reduce of A.
3. At the end whoever has trust point N-1 will be the Judge

TC : O(n) n = number of vertex.
SC : O(n) n number of vertex.
